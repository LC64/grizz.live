The Process

Grizz has a simple technique when it comes to fetch. When the ball is thrown he will attempt to retrieve it as fast as possible. His insane determination is what sets him apart from other dogs.

1

Finds the ball

Grizz almost constantly keeps an eye out for a ball or another suitable object that can be used for fetch.

2

Brings the ball to you

Whether prompted or not, Grizz will bring you the ball. Day or night, rain or shine, Grizz is always ready to play.

3

Waits for you to throw the ball

If another client presents themselves Grizz may take his ball to them, but rest asured he will at some point return to you again, ready to play.

4

Gets the ball

If you do throw the ball, Grizz will go get the ball and he will bring it back. This is a guarantee.
